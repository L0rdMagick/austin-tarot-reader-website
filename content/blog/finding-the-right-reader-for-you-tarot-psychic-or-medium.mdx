## A Quick Recap of the Roles

We've explored these paths in depth, but let's quickly review the core function of each:

-   **The Tarot Reader:** A master of symbols and stories. They use the 78 cards of the tarot deck as a framework to map out the energies, challenges, and potential outcomes surrounding your situation. It's a strategic and empowering tool. For a deep dive, check out our **[guide to Austin's best tarot readers](/blog/austins-best-tarot-readers-complete-guide-atx)**.
-   **The Psychic:** A direct intuitive. They tune into your energy field to receive direct information through their innate senses (seeing, hearing, feeling). A **[psychic reading](/blog/psychic-readings-in-austin-connecting-with-spiritual-guides)** can provide potent, unfiltered insight.
-   **The Spiritual Medium:** A bridge to the spirit world. Their specific skill is connecting with the consciousness and personality of those who have passed on to deliver messages of healing and love. We explain this unique gift in our post, **[Spiritual Mediums vs. Psychics](/blog/spiritual-mediums-vs-psychics-whats-the-real-difference)**.

## Match Your Question to the Reader

The easiest way to decide is to look at the question you want to ask.

**Choose a TAROT READER when you're asking:**

-   *"What are the pros and cons of this decision?"*
-   *"What is the underlying dynamic in my relationship?"*
-   *"What steps can I take to achieve my career goals?"*
-   *"What is the story of my current situation?"*

A tarot reading is perfect for understanding complex situations with many moving parts and for crafting a proactive path forward.

**Choose a PSYCHIC when you're asking:**

-   *"Is this person's energy right for me?"*
-   *"What is the main blockage I'm not seeing?"*
-   *"I have a strong gut feeling about this—what does it mean?"*
-   *"What is the most important message my higher self has for me right now?"*

A psychic reading is ideal for energetic assessments and direct, high-level intuitive hits.

**Choose a MEDIUM when you're asking:**

-   *"Does my departed loved one have a message for me?"*
-   *"Is my grandfather at peace?"*
-   *"I feel my mother's presence; can you help me understand what she's trying to say?"*

A mediumship reading is specifically for healing grief and connecting with those in the spirit world.
