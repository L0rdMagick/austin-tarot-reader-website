For a long time, the world of science and the world of spirituality were kept in strictly separate boxes. But as we dive deeper into the mysteries of **quantum physics**, those boxes are starting to blur. 

One of the most mind-bending concepts in modern science is **quantum entanglement**—the phenomenon where two particles become linked, so that the state of one instantly influences the state of the other, no matter how far apart they are. Einstein famously called this "spooky action at a distance." To a tarot reader, this sounds less like a scientific anomaly and more like a description of a psychic connection.

## Synchronicity and the Observer Effect

In quantum mechanics, the **Observer Effect** suggests that the mere act of observing a particle changes its behavior. In tarot, the act of "observing" your life through the cards shifts your perspective and, subsequently, your future path. 

This mirrors the [Jungian concept of synchronicity](/blog/the-psychology-of-tarot-carl-jung-archetypes)—the idea that the universe provides meaningful coincidences to help us understand our internal state. When you pull the [Death card](/blog/the-death-card-decoded-rebirth-transformation) at the exact moment you were contemplating a major life change, it isn't just luck; it's a synchronous alignment of intent and information.
