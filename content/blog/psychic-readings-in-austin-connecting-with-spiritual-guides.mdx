## What's the Difference Between a Tarot and a Psychic Reading?

This is a great question. Often, the two overlap. Many of **[Austin's best tarot readers](/blog/austins-best-tarot-readers-complete-guide-atx)** are profoundly psychic, using the cards as a tool to focus their intuitive abilities.

However, a "psychic reading" can also refer to a session where the guide relies purely on their innate gifts—like clairvoyance (clear seeing), clairaudience (clear hearing), or empathy—without any tools at all. It's about finding the style that resonates most deeply with you.
